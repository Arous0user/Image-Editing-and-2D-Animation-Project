Topic 3: Retouching & Manipulation

Background

Retouching Tools

Clone Stamp: Copies pixels from one area of an image to another.

Healing Brush: Paints with sampled pixels from an image, matching the texture, lighting, and shading of the sampled pixels to the source pixels.

Patch Tool: Replaces a selected area with pixels from another part of the image, blending the edges.

Creative Editing

These tools are not just for fixing imperfections. They can be used for creative tasks like removing unwanted objects, replacing backgrounds, and other photo manipulations.
